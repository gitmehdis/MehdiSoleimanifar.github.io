Biography

I am an AWS Quantum Postdoctoral Scholar in the Institute for Quantum Information and Matter at Caltech.

I am interested in the interface of quantum information, theoretical computer science, and many-body physics.

My PhD thesis:

Efficiently learning, testing, and simulating quantum many-body systems
